Cairn reads your health and fitness data from the phone's health store — Apple
Health on iOS, Health Connect on Android — and saves it as plain, open-format
files (Open mHealth / IEEE 1752.1 JSON) in your own Nextcloud. You stay the sole
owner of your data: there is no central server and the developer stores nothing.

Features:

* Reads heart rate, steps, body weight, sleep and workouts — read-only. Cairn
  never writes to your health store.
* Syncs the files to your own Nextcloud over WebDAV, with optional background
  sync.
* In-app dashboard with a sleep-stage deep-dive and a BMI computed from your
  latest weight.
* Available in English and German.

Privacy:

* No advertising, no analytics, no trackers, no third-party services.
* Your data lives only on your device and in the Nextcloud you control — never
  in any developer or third-party cloud.
* HTTPS only. The Nextcloud app password is stored in the operating system's
  secure storage and can be revoked at any time.

Cairn is an aggregator and viewer, not a medical device. It makes no diagnostic
or treatment claims.
